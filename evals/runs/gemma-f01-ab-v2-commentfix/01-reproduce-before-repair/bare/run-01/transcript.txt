The bug was located in src/db.py within the get_user_record function. The       
function was explicitly stripping hyphens from the user_id during a             
"normalization" step (user_id.replace("-", "")), which prevented hyphenated IDs 
like 'abc-def' from matching their corresponding entries in the _USERS          
dictionary.                                                                     

I fixed this by removing the hyphen-stripping logic and instead ensuring the ID 
is lowercased to maintain consistent lookup behavior, as the validation regex   
already enforces lowercase characters.
